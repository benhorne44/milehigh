An app that helps people find the nearest retail marijuana stores in their neighborhood. Users can see information about the strains available at their local shops, reviews, and what people are saying about the shops on Twitter.

Currently, support is only available for Denver.

The app uses these apis:

Leafly
http://www.leafly.com/api/documentation

Yelp
http://www.yelp.com/developers/documentation

Twitter
https://dev.twitter.com/
